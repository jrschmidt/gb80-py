 The format for numeric op expressions:                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                             
  The tokens passed to _build_num_op() will always begin with "<numeric_expression>" and end with "<numeric_expression_end>". Since we have already filtered 
   out numeric singleton expressions before _build_num_op() is ever invoked, the expressions, if correct and valid, will always be of the form [term] plus   
  [operand] plus [term], recursively, expressed sequentially in the tokens list and in nested form in the BasicLine object.                                  
                                                                                                                                                             
  If it were not for the parentheses, represented by the tokens "<left_paren>" and "<right_paren>", we could simply search for the first operand of the      
  lowest order available, and divide the tokens at that point into `left`, `right` sub-segments, which we would then evaluate recursively as separate terms.
